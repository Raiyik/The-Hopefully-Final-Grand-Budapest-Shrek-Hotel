About the Creator of "Shrek School"

Shrek, the creator of Shrek school, felt that in the current world,
people lacked knowledge about Shrek and his other monster friends.
Thus, Shrek school was formed by him and his fellow associates to help
educated the general population about the great man that is Shrek.
